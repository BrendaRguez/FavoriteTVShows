My Favorite TV Shows

Introduction

Welcome to my personal list of favorite TV shows! Here, I've gathered some of the series that have made me laugh, cry, and binge-watch for hours. Dive in to see what I love about each one!

Top 5 favorite TV shows

  • The Good Place
  • Gyeongseong Creature
  • Succession
  • The White Lotus
  • Game of Thrones

The Good Place

The Good Place follows Eleanor Shellstrop, who mistakenly ends up in a perfect afterlife despite her flawed past. Determined to earn her spot, she navigates moral lessons and hilarious twists with new friends, questioning what it truly means to be good.

Streaming on Netflix

Gyeongseong Creature

Gyeongseong Creature follows Jang Tae-sang and Yoon Chae-ok as they battle monstrous creatures in 1945 Korea, uncovering dark truths amid a fight for survival. This thrilling series blends horror, history, and suspense.

Streaming on Netflix

Succession

Succession follows the powerful Roy family, owners of a global media empire, as they navigate fierce internal battles over who will inherit control. With sharp wit and intense drama, the series explores loyalty, betrayal, and ambition within one of the world's wealthiest families.

Streaming on Max

The White Lotus

The White Lotus is a dark comedy-drama that follows the lives of wealthy guests and the complex staff at a luxury resort. Set in stunning locations, each season unravels hidden tensions, secrets, and unexpected conflicts, exposing the chaos lurking beneath paradise's surface.

Streaming on Max

Game Of Thrones

Game of Thrones is an epic fantasy saga set in the land of Westeros, where noble families vie for control of the Iron Throne. With alliances, betrayals, and fierce battles, the series follows heroes and villains as they navigate political intrigue and looming supernatural threats, all while striving for power in a brutal world.

Streaming on Max
